Frequently Asked Questions

The migration timeline depends on the complexity of your systems and data volume. We start with a discovery phase to assess your environment and define a realistic, tailored timeline.

Costs vary based on the scope, AWS services used and level of complexity. We provide a detailed cost estimate after an initial assessment. In general, costs include migration planning, execution, and AWS usage during and after the migration.

We follow a step-by-step migration plan with testing, backups, and fallback strategies to keep your systems running smoothly. Whenever possible, we migrate in phases or outside of business hours to avoid downtime and ensure a seamless transition.

We provide ongoing AWS management and optimization to ensure your systems remain secure, efficient, and cost-effective after migration. We offer services tailored to your needs, including proactive monitoring, performance tuning, security updates, and compliance assistance.

We follow industry best practices and AWS's security framework, including encryption, access controls, and regular security audits.

After migration, we conduct thorough testing and validation to ensure all systems are functioning correctly. We provide training and support as needed and offer ongoing maintenance and optimization services.
