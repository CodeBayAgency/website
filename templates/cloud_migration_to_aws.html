Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about cloud migration

The timeline for cloud migration varies based on the complexity and scope of your current IT environment. Simple migrations might take a few weeks, while complex enterprise-level transitions could take several months. We use a phased approach that allows for iterative migrations, so you'll see progress throughout the migration cycle.

Data security is a top priority during migration. We use secure transfer methods, encryption, and strict access controls. We also conduct rigorous testing and validation at each stage. We adhere to industry best practices and AWS security standards to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of your data.

We offer comprehensive post-migration support to ensure a smooth transition. This includes system monitoring, performance tuning, troubleshooting, and security updates. We also provide training and documentation to help your team adapt to the new environment. We can provide ongoing managed services to optimize your AWS infrastructure, handle maintenance, and continue to help you innovate.

Custom software is typically priced based on the complexity, features, and development time required. We provide detailed estimates after a thorough discovery process where we understand your requirements in depth. Our pricing models include fixed-price for well-defined projects and time-and-materials for more flexible development needs. We're transparent about costs and work within your budget constraints while ensuring quality isn't compromised.
